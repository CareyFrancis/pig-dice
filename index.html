PIG DICE

Welcome to Pig Dice! Dare to try test your luck at the game and find out if you are worthy.

Instructions on How To Play.

Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 6 is rolled or the player decides to hold:

  • If the player rolls a 6, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues.
  • If a player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
